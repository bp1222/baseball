# Optional spring league images

To show animated GIFs for Grapefruit and Cactus League series, add:

- **grapefruit.gif** – shown for Grapefruit League (Florida) spring training series
- **cactus.gif** – shown for Cactus League (Arizona) spring training series

Recommended size: about 24–32px. If these files are missing, small SVG icons are used instead.
